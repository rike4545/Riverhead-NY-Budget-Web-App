Invocation given 
IV.  Town Historian Georgette Case - Celebration of Town of Riverhead's 
234th Birthday 
The Town Historian gave an account of the town's 234th birthday. Elaborated with 
all the celebrations that have occurred within the town over the past decades. 
Supervisor Halpin read the proclamation that created the township after Southold 
relinquished the western half of their township. March 7,1792.  
V.  Proclamation - Riverhead Town Justice - Honorable Lori Hulse 
Member of council Merrifield read a proclamation honoring Town Justice Hulse 
during Woman's history month.  
VI.  Women's History Month - Certificates of Appreciation 
Certificates of appreciation are given to all the elected women and department 
heads within the town honoring them during Woman's history month.  
VII.  Announcements

would be held on March 28, at 1:00 in Jamesport. An after party at George Young 
Community Center.
