III.  Public Hearings 
 ● 4:00 PM Qualified and Eligible Public Hearing - The Jazz Loft Inc.  
Daniel Hurley presented an overview and reason for the hearing. 18 Peconic Ave, 
Vail-Levitte music hall. 
The directors of the Jazz Loft present to showcase their ability and desire to 
undertake the renovation and creation of a revitalized performing arts center. 
Presented funding and ability to raise funding to accomplish their mission.  
The accountant representing the jazz loft gave an overview of finances and funding 
sources.  
Brian Stark, a community member, spoke in favor and reiterated the qualifications 
and commitment of the jazz loft. 
Several speakers came to advocate for the jazz loft and support their vision and 
ability. 
PUBLIC COMMENTS 
Brian DeLuca from Eastend Tourism Alliance, and Director of the Atlantis 
Aquarium spoke to support and remind the board of the benefits of active 
performing arts in the Vail-Leavitt.  
IV.  Adjournment 
Hearing closed and held open for written comments till Oct. 24th.
