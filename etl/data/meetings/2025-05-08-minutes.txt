TB Resolution 2025-418 
 
CLASSIFIES SPECIAL PERMIT APPLICATION FOR NATIONAL GRID FACILITY 
UPGRADES AS AN UNLISTED ACTION,  ISSUES NEGATIVE DECLARATION 
PURSUANT TO SEQRA  8 MILL ROAD, RIVERHEAD, NY SCTM NO. 600- 120-2-7 
 
Councilman Rothwell offered the following resolution, 
which was seconded by Councilman Kern 
 
WHEREAS, the Town Board is in receipt of a Special Permit application, 
submitted by National Grid, seeking to redevelop an existing National Grid CNG facility 
located at 8 Mill Road, Riverhead, NY, more particularly described as SCTM No. 600 -
120-2-7; and 
 
 WHEREAS, proposed redevelopment includes  the demolition of two existing 
buildings, demolition of certain utility structures, constructing a new access driveway 
and apron on Mill Road, creating trailer parking spaces for a total of twenty -four (24) 
CNG trucks, and installing related CNG distribution and collection equipment; and  
 
 WHEREAS, the subject property is located within the Peconic River Community 
(PRC) zoning use district; and  
 
 WHEREAS, pursuant to Town Code §301 -225B, public utility buildings and 
structures shall be permitted in all districts when approved by Special Permit of the 
Town Board upon a finding by the Board that adequate buffers exist to minimize 
impacts upon adjacent parcels and taking into account, among other things, the existing 
and permitted uses in the surrounding community; and  
 
WHEREAS, the project site is located within the New York State Department of 
Environmental Conservation Wild, Scenic, and Recreational Rivers Area (Community 
designation); and 
 
 WHEREAS, pursuant to 6NYCRR Part 666.13(H)(1) and (2), no permit is needed 
from the NYSDEC; and  
 
 WHEREAS, the proposed action is an Unlisted Action pursuant to SEQRA, with 
coordinated review being optional and not recommended; and  
 
 WHEREAS, Planning Staff has reviewed the Short Environmental Assessment 
Form (SEAF) Part 1, dated February 13, 2025, and has prepared SEAF Parts 2 and 3, 
dated April 28, 2025 (attached). 
 
NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED,  that the Town Board hereby issues a 
Negative Declaration pursuant to SEQRA for the Special Permit application of National 
Grid Facility Upgrades, finding that the proposed action is not expected to result in any 
significant negative environmental impacts; and be it further

TB Resolution 2025-420 
 
RESOLUTION FOR HOME RULE REQUEST TO THE STATE LEGISLATURE IN 
SUPPORT OF BILL DRAFT 04421-03-5 AND FISCAL NOTE #2025-154 ISSUED 
MAY 5, 2025 FOR “AN ACT GRANTING RETROACTIVE TIER II MEMBERSHIP IN 
THE NEW YORK STATE AND LOCAL POLICE AND FIRE RETIREMENT SYSTEM 
TO GIUSEPPE T. ROSINI” 
 
Councilwoman Merrifield offered the following resolution, 
which was seconded by Councilwoman Waski 
 
WHEREAS, Giuseppe T. Rosini has been a member of the Town of Riverhead 
Police Department and member of the New York State and Local Police and Fire 
Retirement System since December 15, 2014 with a Tier VI status; and 
 
WHEREAS, notwithstanding the above, at the time of his employment with 
Wading River Fire Department in October of 2004 and throughout his employment 
ending in December of 2008 with the Wading River Fire Department, Giuseppe T. 
Rosini, was eligible to enroll as a member in the New York State and Local Police and 
Fire Retirement System as a Tier II status member; and 
 
WHEREAS, due to an error or mistake by and acknowledged by Wading River 
Fire Department, Giuseppe T. Rosini was not notified of his eligibility to enroll, nor 
offered the opportunity to enroll based upon his part -time employment with Wading 
River Fire Department; and 
  
WHEREAS, in April of 2021, the Wading River Fire District communicated with 
New York State & Local Retirement System, in writing, setting forth Giuseppe T. 
Rosini’s employment history with Wading River Fire District, and most significantly the 
error regarding the failure to offer him enrollment in the New York State and Local 
Police and Fire Retirement System as a Tier II member, and 
 
WHEREAS, in addition to service in the New York State and Local Police and 
Fire Retirement System beginning prior to 2004 and for more than two decades, 
Giuseppe T. Rosini has volunteered or through paid service, served the branches of 
Fire Department or Emergency Medical Team, and 
 
WHEREAS, in addition to the above, Giuseppe T. Rosini’s dedication and 
commitment to the community is exemplified by awards, recognitions and 
commendations related to his employment and volunteer work. 
 
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED , that the Town Board of the Town of 
Riverhead hereby supports and approves a home rule request for Bill Draft 04421 -03-5 
And Fiscal Note #2025-154 Issued May 5, 2025 For “An Act Granting Retroactive Tier II 
Membership in The New York State and Local Police and Fire Retirement System to 
Giuseppe T. Rosini”, and
