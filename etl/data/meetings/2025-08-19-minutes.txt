VIII.  Public Hearings 
 ● 6:00 PM - Public Hearing - Amendment Chapter 301, Art.XLV  
Open 6:16, Matthew Charters gave background and amendments closed at 6:18 
open for written 8/29 
 ● 6:00 PM - Public Hearing - Moratorium Cannabis Establishments  
Open 6:18; Matthew Charters gave an overview of Amendment to Cannabis 
Chapter 304. Closed and held open for written comment till 8/29/25. Several 
community members supported a moratorium. The owner of a cannabis store 
spoke against a moratorium, stating the town is nowhere near saturation. Spirited 
debate on moratorium and legal use of cannabis. 
 ● 6:00 PM - Public Hearing - Amendment Chapter 301, Art.XVIII, Business Center  
Open 7:18, Matthew Charters gave an overview of the amendment. Closed at 7:19, 
written comment till 8/29/25 
 ● 6:00 PM - Public Hearing - Amendment Chapter 301, Art.XXII, Shopping Center  
Open at 7:20, Matthew Charters gave an overview of the amendment. Closed at 
7:23, held open for written comment till 8/29/25 
 ● 6:05 PM - Public Hearing - Monroe Balancing Test - Suff Co Water Ext  
TA Eric Howard gave an overview of the Monroe balancing test, for the SCWA 
extension through the town. Matthew Charters gave an overview. Jeff Seaman, an 
environmental consultant working for the town, gave an overview of how little 
information they have as to where exactly the proposed eight-mile transmission 
line will be located. The SCWA representative spoke about the authority of the 
Water District and how regionally it has significant and deliberate responsibilities to 
the residents within their service areas. Highly debated issue on impacts and 
remedies, and future conversations. Community members present were speaking 
of the impacts and no benefits to the township or communities affected. Numerous 
Zoom calls all stated the same concerns. Several statements were presented in 
the record. 
 ● 6:10 PM - Public Hearing - Amendment Chapter 289, Vehicle, Traffic and 
Parking  
DTA Pilo gave an overview of the amendment to the town code. Opened at 8:57 
PM, Closed at 8:59, written comments till 8/29/25 
 ● 6:15 PM - Public Hearing - Amendment Chapter 231, Art.XXIV, Fire Prevention  
DTA Pilo gave an update to the amendment . Closed, written comment 8/29/25 
 ● 6:20 PM - Public Hearing - Amendment Chapter 251, Art.I, Noise  
TA Howard gave an overview of the amendment to the town code. Opened at 9:00, 
closed at 9:03, held open for written comment till 8/29/25.
