VII.  Public Scoping Session  
 ● Special Permit and Site Plan Application of Duffy MX Motorcross Track 2822 
River Road Calverton, NY SCTM NO. 600-136-1-4  
The scoping hearing started at 6:07 pm. Planner Greg Bergman gave an overview 
of the site plan and contemplated development.  
Laura Jens Smith, Laurel, Thanked the board for taking positive steps to address 
environmental issues. Gave a list of additional scopes the board should consider.  
Victor Prusinowski, representing the developer, stated they will mitigate and work 
with the town and DEC. 
Barbara Blass reiterated previously stated concerns about environmental issues.  
Dan Duffy, the applicant, questioned the downside of the indoor track, lighting and 
air quality.  
Numerous speakers were concerned about noise and noise mitigation.  
Calverton Civic read concerns into record and will supply a letter to be circulated.
