VII.  Public Hearings 
 ● 6:15 PM - Public Hearing - Rental Dwelling Units  
The Public Hearing was called to order at 6:16, Town Attorney Erik Howard gave 
an overview of the amendment to the rental dwelling units code. Several speakers 
from the community spoke on issues with transient rentals and short-term rentals. 
Several citizens spoke of possibly reducing short-term rentals to 14 days as 
opposed to the current 29 days.  
Zoom—Bob Klein agrees with the code amendment and quality of life issues. 
Zoom — Mike Jones — currently rents his home, 30 days at a time. Concerned 
about the extreme measures with regard to penalties. 
Closed at 7:14, Nov 28th for written comments
