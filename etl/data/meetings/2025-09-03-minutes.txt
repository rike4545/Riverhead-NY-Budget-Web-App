VII.  Public Hearings 
 ● 2:00 PM - Public Hearing - Chapter 217, Unsafe Structure  
Opened at 2:09, Deputy Town Attorney Danielle Hurley gave an overview and 
called code enforcement and engineering to give testimony. 
Testimony given to the condition and attempts to get the owner to secure and 
make the building safe. Exhibits were presented and testified too, and made part of 
the record. Town Engineer Kenneth Testa gave a detailed engineering report as to 
the structural condition of the building, complete with photographs. Neighbors to 
the property spoke as to the history and length of complaints about the property. 
Public Hearing closed, not held open for written comment, at 3:37 pm 
 ● 2:05 PM - Public Hearing - Amendment Chapter 301, Art.LVI, Site Plan Review  
Open at 3:38, Deputy Pilo gave an overview of code amendments and fee 
increases. No public comment.Closed at 3:39, held open till September 13th.
